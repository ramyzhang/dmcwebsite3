About Us

The Deezed Man Collective (DMC) is a music collective born on the suburban outskirts of Toronto, Ontario. Influenced by an eclectic and groovy fusion of rock, blues, jazz, R&B, pop, and a dash of progressive metal, our fresh sound can get any crowd to jam along.

Members of the DMC have performed at the Beaches International Jazz Festival, toured throughout China, and performed at venues across Southern Ontario (2019 Aurora Winter Festival at the CNE, DY Live Music Bar, UWaterloo Alumni Day 2020) and their own show at Smiling Buddha for Leap Year 2020.

Before we were deezed...

It all started in the early 2000s, when childhood friends Anthony Nufrio (guitars & songwriting) and Matthew Reyes (drums & production) picked their first instruments in grade school music class. Starting out with their favourite songs and soundtracks, they developed an amazing chemistry playing together.

University put them on separate paths for years, until they re-united and began the DMC in 2019. Since then, the collective has grown to include Ramy Zhang on vocals, and John Salazar on keys.

And this is still not yet our final form - the DMC continues to grow and collaborate with music-making folks from all across the Internet, starting from smooth R&B covers with @kundimusic and going to dark hip hop with @vibebymelo and many more. We're on a funk-filled journey to one day to be the most deezed collective of all collectives.
