About Us

The Deezed Man Collective (DMC) is a music collective born on the suburban outskirts of Toronto, Ontario. Influenced by an eclectic and groovy fusion of rock, blues, jazz, R&B, pop, and a dash of progressive metal, our fresh sound can get any crowd to jam along.

Members of the DMC have performed at the Beaches International Jazz Festival, toured throughout China, and performed at events across Southern Ontario - most recently for the 2019 Aurora Winter Festival at Ontario Place.

Before we were deezed...

It all started in the mid-2000s, when childhood friends Anthony Nufrio, Matthew Reyes, and Kyle Palmer picked up their first instruments in grade school music class. Starting with favorite songs, then slowly fiddling with video game soundtracks, the group developed an amazing chemistry together.

College and university put them on separate paths for years, until they came back together with new friends again to create the DMC in 2019. Since then, the collective has evolved and shifted, and today, the core group of DMC consists of Anthony Nufrio on guitar and vocals, Matthew Reyes on drums, Ramy Zhang on vocals, and John Salazar on keys.

And still this is not yet our final form - DMC is much bigger than just these four members! Collaborating with music-making folks from all across the world, the DMC is on a funk-filled journey to one day build the most deezed collective of all music collectives.
